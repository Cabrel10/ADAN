📋 QUALITY SCORE CALCULATION
================================================================================

Formula:
Quality = (Win_Rate × 40%) + (Profit_Factor × 30%) + (Trade_Count × 20%) + (Avg_PnL × 10%)

Components:
1. Win Rate (40%) - Percentage of profitable trades
2. Profit Factor (30%) - Ratio of gross profit to gross loss

4. Avg PnL (10%) - Mean profit/loss per trade
