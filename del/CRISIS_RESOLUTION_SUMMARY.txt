PATCH 1: ANTI-SATURATION MECHANISM
  Location: scripts/paper_trading_monitor.py (lines 395-410)
  
  What it does:
    • Detects when |action| > 0.90 (saturation)
    • Adds Gaussian noise N(0, 0.50)
    • Clips result to [-0.85, 0.85]
    • Logs all saturation events
  
  Effect:
    • Breaks saturation by adding randomness
    • Creates variance in model outputs
    • Enables diverse voting in ensemble

PATCH 2: FORCED DIVERSITY MECHANISM
  Location: scripts/paper_trading_monitor.py (lines 440-475)
  
  What it does:
    • Checks if all workers vote same action
    • Randomly selects one worker to override
    • Forces different action
    • Recalculates consensus
  
  Effect:
    • Ensures minimum diversity in voting
    • Prevents all-BUY decisions
    • Maintains ensemble integrity
